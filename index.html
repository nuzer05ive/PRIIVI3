About Me

I'm Nicholas Rood, an independent researcher specializing in recursive optimization, number theory, and the intersection of physics, AI, and mathematics.

My work focuses on harmonic recursion, prime number distribution, and their fundamental links to quantum mechanics and relativity. I aim to uncover the deep mathematical structures underlying reality through the Recursive Fractal Retrograde Model (RFRM) and related theories.
